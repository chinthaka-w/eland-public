Services

The following e-services are offered through the eLand public portal

  • Request for new notary registration
  • Request to obtain the notary license in a different language
  • Request to change the new judicial division of a notary
  • Request to change the name of a notary in the license
  • Search folios
  • Search deeds
  • Request to obtain extract of folios
  • Request to obtain extract of deed

E-Land

The objective of e-Land is to provide a solution to ensure convenient access to land registers, registration information, facilitate managing of land information while ensuring the safety of documents by providing a speedy, convenient and economical service to general public.
